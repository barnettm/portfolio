ABOUT ME

Hi there, I'm Matt - a lover of technology, design, dogs and tacos! After discovering a passion for web development, I couldn't get enough. I used every resource available to learn development and design, and jumped on every opportunity to build or contribute to a project. My specialty is Front End Development with a particular fondness for React and JavaScript. I love to solve challenging problems and bring creative, elegant and user-friendly applications to the world.


I recently completed a full immersion Web Development Program in Irvine, Ca, where I had the opportunity to develop several applications, as well as participate in several hackathon competitions.
